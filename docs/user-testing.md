# User Testing Results

Tried it out today. Here are my notes:

1. 25 minutes is too much for most timers, and it takes too long/too many taps to get to one minute.
2. The display of the time configuration is messed up.
3. iOS still seems to quit the app after a couple of minutes. Maybe I need to use a live activity?
4. The original goal was to stop looking at the phone, and be confident that the app will trigger a sound or buzz on time, but if I don't trust that it will work, I end up opening my phone and checking the app all the time.
5. The watch component is not working well at all.
6. In practice, the watch isn't that big a deal. I'm wearing airpods, and the phone is in my pocket, so that's a better way to get the "bip".
7. The sound is more important than the haptic buzz. A lot of time, I didn't even notice the haptic.
8. I would like to play music, but I think the music would need to be ducked before playing the "bip" sound.
9. The app design is very stale/utilitarion. It defintely feels like Jony Ive's Apple and not Steve's Apple. I want me some brushed metal!
